**Game Type**  
Multiplayer, Free-For All, Deathmatch, with Inventory/Crafting, and basic block-based building.

**Overview**  
In this multiplayer game, citizens are forced to survive in a corrupt police state that is ruthlessly controlled by the Mayor and his Artificial Police Force (APF). The citizens trapped in this forsaken city are forced to find a hideout somewhere in the city and setup a base where they can hide their drug labs which is the main source of income for players to buy weapons and building material.  If you don’t have enough money to start building out your base you can hunt zombies to scavenge their bodies for resources.  Ultimately when you finally get sick of the Mayor’s constant assault on the citizens of this city, it will be up to you to assassinate the Mayor to take control this corrupted city.  
   
**Take Control of the City**  
As a citizen you can invade the city core, assassinate the acting Mayor, and take control of the city … for as long as you can hold it.  As the “new” mayor of the city you can assert your control and profit immensely by oppressing your citizens by deploying Artificial Police Force (APF).  At the command center in the city core you’ll have the ability to deploy APF to areas of the city to remind citizens who's in charge.  Make sure you don’t forget to keep reserve APF in the city core to repel assaults.

**Fight Back the Artificial Police Force**  
While you’re setting up your miserable existence you’ll also have to worry about frequent deployments of AFP from the acting Mayor attempting to remind you who is in control. The streets will quickly become flooded with AFP assaults, but are you going to let some "Artificial" tell you what to do?

**Build Your Drug Fueled Empire**  
There are no jobs here in this city, it's a dog eat dog world so you are forced to make money anyway you can which includes setting up drug labs, destroying other people's drug labs, and hunting zombies.  As you gain money you will be able to finance weapons from the weapon dealer which will allow you to assault the city core and take control of the city. Let's see how long you can keep control.

**Destroy Competing Drug Labs**  
As the competition for drugs increases the income you earn will decrease due to competition.  In order to keep profits up you'll need to find other citizen's drug labs throughout the city and destroy them.  Upon destruction you immediately get a cash reward and your passive income will start increasing.

**Hunt and Loot Zombies**  
The dead fall to the sewers where citizens can hunt zombies for whatever resources may have left in their clothes.  If you have nothing this is a great way to start up again.

**Create Your Hideout**  
Citizens can buy the door to a room or warehouse and lock/unlock it allowing them to build and defend a base inside either alone or with friends.  Beware players can get a hold of lockpicks to break in and destroy your hideout.

**Persistent Inventory for Next Time...**  
Everything you earn during your time in city is saved in your inventory until the next time you join allowing for a more "explosive" gameplay.
